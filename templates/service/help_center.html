Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click "Get Started Free" on the homepage, enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. No personal information is required beyond your email.

Is ShadowTalk really anonymous?

Yes! We don't require personal information, and chat partners only see your display name. We don't store chat logs permanently, and your identity remains protected.

What are the system requirements?

ShadowTalk works on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and mobile devices. For voice chat, you'll need a microphone and speakers/headphones.

Account & Profile

How do I change my display name?

Go to your profile settings, click "Edit Profile," and update your display name. You can change it as often as you like.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can permanently delete your account in the account settings. This will remove all your data from our systems.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

Chatting Features

How does the matching system work?

Our AI matches you with strangers based on shared interests and conversation patterns. You can also use filters to find specific types of chat partners.

Can I use voice chat?

Yes! Once connected with a partner, click the microphone icon to request voice chat. Both users need to consent to start voice communication.

What are suggested replies?

Our AI analyzes conversations and suggests relevant responses to keep chats flowing smoothly. These are optional and you're free to type your own messages.

Safety & Privacy

How do I report a user?

Click the report button in the chat interface, select the violation type, and provide details. Our safety team reviews reports promptly.

What happens when I block someone?

Blocked users can no longer message you, see your online status, or be matched with you in future chats.

Is my data secure?

We use end-to-end encryption, don't store chat logs permanently, and follow strict data protection practices. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

Why can't I find a chat partner?

This could be due to low user traffic in your region or strict filters. Try adjusting your search criteria or try again during peak hours.

Why is my voice chat not working?

Check your browser permissions to ensure microphone access is allowed. Also verify that your partner has accepted the voice chat request.

Why are my messages not sending?

Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, refresh the page or try switching browsers.
