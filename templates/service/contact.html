Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

Response times vary by category: Safety concerns (1-2 hours), General support (24-48 hours), Legal inquiries (2-3 business days).

What information should I include in a safety report?

Include the username, approximate time of incident, description of the behavior, and any relevant screenshots (automatically anonymized).

Can I request my data to be deleted?

Yes, you can request account deletion at any time. Visit your account settings or contact privacy@shadowtalk.com for assistance.

How do I report a technical issue?

Use the technical support category in the form above. Include your browser/device information and steps to reproduce the issue.
