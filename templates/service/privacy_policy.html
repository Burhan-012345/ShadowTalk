Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are our top priorities

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for verification and login)
  • Encrypted password
  • Optional display name and basic profile information

1.2 Chat Data

We temporarily process:

  • Chat messages (encrypted during transmission)
Important: Chat messages are not permanently stored and are deleted after chat sessions end. We do not retain conversation history.

1.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP address (for security and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Facilitate anonymous chat connections
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service notifications

2.2 Safety and Moderation

  • Monitor for abusive behavior and content
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Prevent spam and malicious activities

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Improve chat matching algorithms
  • Develop new features

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these circumstances:

3.1 Legal Requirements

When required by law, legal process, or governmental request.

3.2 Safety Emergencies

To prevent imminent harm to individuals or address serious security threats.

3.3 Service Providers

With trusted third-party providers who assist in operating our service, under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for chat messages
  • Secure socket layer (SSL) technology
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited data retention policies

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data

5.2 Communication Preferences

You can opt-out of promotional emails while maintaining essential service communications.

6. Children's Privacy

ShadowTalk is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country, but we ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses and other safeguards.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify users of significant changes through our platform or via email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@shadowtalk.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Page
