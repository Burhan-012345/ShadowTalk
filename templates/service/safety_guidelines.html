Never Share

  • Personal identification
  • Home or work address
  • Financial information
  • Social media accounts
  • Phone numbers
  • Exact location data

Warning Signs

  • Requests for personal information
  • Pressure to move to other platforms
  • Threatening or aggressive language
  • Inappropriate content or requests
  • Suspicious link sharing
  • Attempts to meet in person

Safe Practices

  • Use the platform's anonymity features
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately
  • Block users who make you uncomfortable
  • Trust your instincts
  • Keep conversations on the platform
  • Use strong, unique passwords

Reporting and Blocking

When to Report

Immediately report users who:

  • Send threatening or harassing messages
  • Share inappropriate or explicit content
  • Attempt to obtain personal information
  • Engage in hate speech or discrimination
  • Share spam or malicious links
  • Violate our Terms of Service in any way

How to Report

  • Click the report button in the chat interface
  • Select the appropriate violation category
  • Provide specific details about the incident
  • Submit screenshots if possible (they're automatically anonymized)

Blocking Users

You can block any user at any time. Blocked users cannot:

  • Send you messages
  • See your online status
  • Match with you in future chats

AI Safety Features

Automatic Moderation

Our AI systems work 24/7 to protect you:

  • Real-time toxicity detection
  • Automatic content filtering
  • Suspicious behavior monitoring
  • Pattern recognition for repeat offenders

Privacy Protection

  • Automatic blurring of personal information
  • Encrypted message transmission
  • No permanent chat logs stored
  • Automatic data deletion policies

Mental Health and Well-being

Setting Boundaries

  • Take regular breaks from chatting
  • Set time limits for your sessions
  • Don't feel obligated to continue uncomfortable conversations
  • Remember you can leave any chat at any time

Recognizing Emotional Impact

Online interactions can affect your mental health. Be aware of:

  • Feeling drained or anxious after chats
  • Changes in your mood or sleep patterns
  • Excessive time spent on the platform
  • Difficulty disconnecting from conversations

Seeking Support

If you're experiencing emotional distress:

  • Reach out to trusted friends or family
  • Contact mental health professionals
  • Use crisis hotlines for immediate support
  • Consider taking a break from online platforms

Parental Guidance

ShadowTalk is intended for users 18 years and older. Parents and guardians should:

  • Monitor internet usage of minors
  • Discuss online safety practices
  • Use parental control software
  • Report any underage usage to us immediately

Legal Protection

Remember that online actions have real-world consequences:

  • Threats and harassment are illegal in most jurisdictions
  • Sharing explicit content without consent may violate laws
  • Impersonation and fraud are criminal offenses
  • We cooperate with law enforcement for serious violations

Need Help?

If you have safety concerns or need assistance:

  • Email: safety@shadowtalk.com
  • Use the in-app reporting system
  • Visit our Help Center
  • Contact us through our Contact Page

Response Time: We typically respond to safety reports within 1-2 hours.
